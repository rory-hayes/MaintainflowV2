# Roadmap

## Now: production-ready Business Evals launch

- keep one canonical V2 release line on `rory-hayes/MaintainflowV2` `main`
- complete the additive Business Evals schema, tenant-safe typed APIs, and legacy adapters
- prove Lead form and Trial signup against controlled production fixtures
- complete Browserbase-backed Playwright, durable workflow orchestration, inbound email, private evidence, cleanup, and safety controls
- verify sign-up, confirmation, recovery, Google OAuth, onboarding, billing, quotas, alerts, reports, and revocable sharing on the deployed host
- preserve Legacy endpoint journeys, incidents, reports, entitlements, and rollback evidence
- pass unit, contract, migration, E2E, responsive, accessibility, security, clean-export, and seven-day canary gates
- switch the public domain only after the exact V2 commit and provider-backed canaries pass

## After stable launch evidence

- improve white-labelled Agency reporting and delivery controls
- expand report-safe evidence adapters where buyers demonstrate demand
- improve journey repair suggestions while keeping review and publishing manual
- add higher-volume agency controls and richer portfolio coverage views
- prioritise onboarding and distribution work using measured activation and retention

## Later, only with demand and a new product decision

- additional deterministic business-journey templates
- customer-owned test-data adapters beyond the launch cleanup contract
- ticket-tool integrations
- regional runner capacity and enterprise controls

## Not now

- another rebuild, repository, product shell, or competing production line
- CRM, invoicing, project management, or workflow building
- generic client portal
- arbitrary scripts or general-purpose browser automation
- payments/checkouts, file uploads, CAPTCHA bypassing, or private CRM access
- model-evaluation lab, trace warehouse, model gateway, or marketplace
- autonomous production repair or AI-authored verdicts
- native n8n, Make, or Zapier OAuth integrations without demand evidence
