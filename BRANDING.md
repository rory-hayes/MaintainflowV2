# Branding

## Customer-facing name

Maintain Flow

## Domain and release line

Use **Maintain Flow** in repository guidance, product copy, and deployments. TuesdayOps is a historical donor name only. Customer-facing links must use the configured production domain, normally `https://www.maintainflow.io`, rather than a preview or Vercel URL.

## Launch promise

> Maintain Flow continuously proves that your critical customer journeys still work—from the first page to the final business outcome.

## Supporting messages

Primary:

> Know when a lead form or trial signup stops producing the result your business depends on.

Evidence-focused:

> Deterministic journey checks, reviewable evidence, verified recoveries, and client-ready reports.

Agency-focused:

> Prove the customer journeys you ship still work without manually retesting every site.

## Positioning

Maintain Flow is a Business Evals platform for agencies, SaaS teams, and independent builders. It safely exercises authorized public Lead form and Trial signup journeys, determines results from deterministic rules, preserves stage-level evidence, opens incidents for definitive business failures, and proves repairs with newer passing reruns. Existing endpoint monitors remain clearly labelled Legacy endpoint journeys.

## Truthful claim rules

Do not claim that Maintain Flow is live, production-proven, trusted by customers, revenue-generating, or capable of running a provider-backed browser journey unless the specific deployment and provider path has been verified. Local controlled fixtures must be labelled as product previews or controlled examples, never customer evidence.

Do not say:

- powered by OpenAI or OpenAI-backed
- enterprise AI observability platform
- autonomous QA or autonomous repair
- arbitrary browser automation
- workflow builder
- CRM for agencies
- client portal for agencies
- CAPTCHA bypass or stealth browsing

Avoid or minimise:

- Trace or span
- Judge or scorer
- Prompt eval
